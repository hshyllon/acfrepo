KNOW OUR STORY *

Dr Kola Okuyemi and his wife Funke Okuyemi (from Nigeria) started ACF Kansas city in June 1995 after he run into Dr Fred Lobati (from Cameroon) at a medical Conference in Kansas city. Their conversation quickly shifted from medical to their common desire for Christian Fellowship with other African. They desired to have prayers and worship the African style. So they quickly brought their families together and started a prayer fellowship. The fellowship focused on prayers for African nations , churches in Africa and their family needs.
